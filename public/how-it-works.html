How Dag Works

Find a dog, speak to the breeder, place a bid or complete a secure PayPal purchase.

1️⃣

Browse

Visit breeder profiles and compare available breeds, photographs, prices and locations.

2️⃣

Contact or bid

Ask the breeder about health records, registration, vaccinations and collection arrangements.

3️⃣

Pay securely

PayPal processes the transaction. Dag marks the dog Sold only after a completed payment is confirmed by the server.

Important buyer safety

Meet the breeder where possible, verify identity and records, inspect the dog’s living conditions, and use a written sales agreement. Never pay outside the agreed secure checkout without independently confirming the breeder.
