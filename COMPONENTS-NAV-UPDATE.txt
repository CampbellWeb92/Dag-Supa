Add these links inside the navigation section in assets/components.js:

${navLink('register', 'register.html', 'Register as Breeder')}
${navLink('login', 'login.html', 'Breeder Login')}

Recommended placement: directly after the Contact link.

You may also add these links to the footer:
<a href="register.html">Register as a Breeder</a>
<a href="login.html">Breeder Login</a>
